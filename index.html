About me

Hi! I am Samuel Egbeola, a Google-certified Data Analyst. Proffering solutions to problems through insights-generation from data keeps me excited. I aim to optimize operations, improve efficiency, and curb losses through data-driven strategies. Technically, I am proficient in the use of Spreadsheets, SQL, Tableau, and Python.

Asides data, I enjoy reading about the history of countries, economic development, and finance.

Projects

Here are some of the projects I have worked on. You can click on the "View project" button to interact with the various dashboards.

Tesla Stock Price Analytics (Live)

This analysis was performed to uncover trends in the stock price of Tesla. It showcases the trends in opening and closing prices over the last three months and updates daily.

Sport Products Sales Analysis

The aim of this analysis is to guide potential investors on how to invest the US sport products market. It reveals the sales trends by region, product, and sales channel (online, in-store, or outlet).

Green Destination HR Analysis

An exploratory data analysis on the attrition rate of a company's employees. The visualizations showcase the correlation between employee retention and monthly income, employee age, and years at the company.

Discount Mart Sales Analysis

An analysis project on the sales and profit trends of a chain of supermarkets. The dashboard was designed to display sales trends for the selected year by month, category, and location.

Superstore Sales Agents Analytics

The aim of this project is to track the performance of US-based Sales agents for the famous retailer Superstore. A breakdown of sales per category and subcategory as well as the top-performing products between selected start and end dates.

Bread Basket Bakery Sales Analysis

I did this project to help this fictional bakery cut down on its inefficiencies. This analysis explores the trends in the orders it receives by period of the day, period of the week, month of the year. It also identifies its most frequently purchased items.

Northwind Shipping Analytics

I conducted this analysis to help the shipping manager track the number of orders that have been processed and shipped on a monthly basis. This would help him/her identify the number of outstanding shipments and aid logistic planning.

MyPaper Account Receivable Analysis

MyPaper is a company that supplies paper locally to businesses. This project would help the finance manager track the amount each customer paid on a montly basis, the average days overdue, and the number of invoices disputed to aid his financial planning.
